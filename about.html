About blogpaste

blogpaste.online is a small, independent blog about writing, note-taking, and the unglamorous habits that make both possible. There's no editorial calendar and no team behind it — just short posts, published whenever one is finished, about the process of thinking things through in writing.

What you'll find here

Mostly first-person essays on writing habits, drafting, and the everyday friction of turning a half-formed thought into something readable. New posts appear on the homepage as they're published, newest first, and you can search or filter by topic from there.

Why "blogpaste"

The name is a nod to the part of writing that happens before anything is polished — pasting a rough idea somewhere it can be looked at again, instead of losing it in a notebook that never gets reopened.
