Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This policy explains what information blogpaste.online ("we," "this site") collects, and how it's used. This is a small independently-run blog, and this page is written in plain language rather than dense legal boilerplate — but it is still meant as a real, functioning privacy policy.

Information we collect

We do not require visitors to create an account, and we do not collect personal information simply for reading the blog. If you use the contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you choose to submit, solely to respond to you.

Cookies and similar technologies

This site, and the third parties described below, may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to store information in your browser and to understand how the site is used.

Advertising: Google AdSense

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns, such as which posts are read and how visitors arrive at the site. This data is used in aggregate and is not used to identify individual visitors.

Your choices

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking cookies may affect how ads are personalized, but the site itself will continue to work normally.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally as the site changes. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact page or to hello@blogpaste.online.
